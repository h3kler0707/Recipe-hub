Chole Bhature

Prep Time: 40 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas, soaked and cooked
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, salt, and water into a soft dough for bhature. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Sauté cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chole masala, and cooked chickpeas.
  4. Simmer the chickpea curry until thick and flavorful.
  5. Roll dough into rounds and deep-fry until puffed and golden.
  6. Serve the bhature hot with chole and lemon wedges.
