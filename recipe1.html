Butter Chicken

Prep Time: 40 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt and red chili powder

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger, garlic, chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree, garam masala, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add chicken and simmer until cooked through.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer gently for 3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
