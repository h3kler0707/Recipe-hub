Vegetable Biryani

Prep Time: 50 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro and mint
  • Fried onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak rice for 20 minutes, then parboil and drain.
  2. Sauté onions, ginger-garlic paste, and tomatoes in oil.
  3. Add vegetables, biryani masala, and yogurt; cook until slightly dry.
  4. Layer rice over the vegetable mixture and cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro, mint, and fried onions.
  6. Serve hot with raita or salad.
