Masala Dosa

Prep Time: 45 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and urad dal for 4 hours, then blend into a smooth batter.
  2. Ferment the batter for 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Sauté onions, potatoes, mustard seeds, turmeric, and chili to make the filling.
  4. Spread a thin layer of batter on a hot pan and cook until golden.
  5. Add the potato filling, fold the dosa, and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
