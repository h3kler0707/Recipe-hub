Paneer Tikka Masala

Prep Time: 35 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate paneer with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Sauté onions and bell pepper until soft, then set aside.
  3. Cook tomato puree with garam masala and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add paneer cubes and vegetables to the sauce.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer for another 4 minutes.
  6. Serve with naan or steamed rice.
